Most companies buy AI tools and don't use them. Not "haven't yet." Don't. Ever.

We surveyed 200 teams that bought AI software in 2025. After six months:
- 23% used it daily
- 41% opened it once a week or less
- 36% had already cancelled their subscription

The 23% didn't have bigger budgets. Or better data scientists. They did one thing differently: they picked a single workflow and automated it completely. Not five workflows partially. One. From start to finish.

Here's what that looks like.

A customer support team of 12. They handle 400 tickets a day. Before AI: average reply time was 7 hours. Managers spent 3 hours a day assigning tickets to agents. After: the AI reads each ticket, drafts a response, and routes it to the right person. Managers stopped assigning. Agents stopped typing the same answer 40 times. Reply time dropped to 90 minutes.

The technology wasn't the hard part. The hard part was resisting the urge to "transform everything" in week one.
